Doctrines

Neurodiversity recognizes and respects neurological and cognitive differences as any other human variation.

Neurodiversity comes from Neuro (to do with the brain) and Diversity (range of differences). Our brain is the source of cognition. With approximately 100 billion neurons the brain constructs amazingly complex networks or circuits through which chemical signals and electrical impulses travel to enable information processing. Each individual network, like our fingerprint, is unique.

These individual idiosyncrasies in the neural networks, make each of us interpret the same situation differently. And it these differences that are much more marked in neurodivergent populations.

Neuro divergents are all those individuals whose brain functioning diverges from that of the neuro typical.

Traditionally what we call Neurodivegence is today most often viewed through the lens of a disability, seen as a disorder. This covers a range of neurodiverse conditions like Autism Spectrum Condition, ADHD, Specific Learning Disorder (LSD), Mood Disorders like Anxiety Disorder, Bipolar, Major Depressive Disorder, OCD, Dyspraxia, Tourette, Synesthesia, Giftedness and many others.

Each Neurodivergence Type, although distinct, has overlapping symptoms and they often co-exist; it is difficult to categorize them into watertight compartments.

Today what we have realized is that these neuro-divergences or neurodiverse conditions have much more to offer. With the perceived deficits, also come a lot of gifts, that are possibly submerged but with the right environment they can be unleashed and when we do that, we create MAGIC!!

Orchvate Proposes Neurodiversity Doctrine Triad

  • Avoid over emphasis on labels.
  • Neuro-divergent Individuals have the right to be the way they are different.
  • Create an empathetic setting that accommodates and encourages all to work to their full potential.
