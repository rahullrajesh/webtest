About Us

Orchvate offers an innovative approach to build a neurodiverse and inclusive society, where boundaries are broken and we collaborate to create wonders. At Orchvate we are a neurodiverse team of psychologists (clinical & organizational) and neurodivergents who have chosen to unmask and self-advocate for the cause.

We have the professional expertise to deal with neuro-divergents, recognize their strengths, match them to the right roles and integrate them into the workplace because we understand the challenges they are likely to face.

Alongside, we have the professional expertise to partner with enterprises, understand hiring requirements, coach ND resources and enable right fits to achieve organizational goals.

Orchvate best collaborates with both entities and bridges the gap to build the neuro-diverse workplace.

Doctrines

Neurodiversity recognizes and respects neurological and cognitive differences as any other human variation.

`

Neurodiversity comes from Neuro (to do with the brain) and Diversity (range of differences). Our brain is the source of cognition. With approximately 100 billion neurons the brain constructs amazingly complex networks or circuits through which chemical signals and electrical impulses travel to enable information processing. Each individual network, like our fingerprint, is unique.

These individual idiosyncrasies in the neural networks, make each of us interpret the same situation differently. And it these differences that are much more marked in neurodivergent populations.

Neuro divergents are all those individuals whose brain functioning diverges from that of the neuro typical.

Traditionally what we call Neurodivegence is today most often viewed through the lens of a disability, seen as a disorder. This covers a range of neurodiverse conditions like Autism Spectrum Condition, ADHD, Specific Learning Disorder (LSD), Mood Disorders like Anxiety Disorder, Bipolar, Major Depressive Disorder, OCD, Dyspraxia, Tourette, Synesthesia, Giftedness and many others.

Each Neurodivergence Type, although distinct, has overlapping symptoms and they often co-exist; it is difficult to categorize them into watertight compartments.

Today what we have realized is that these neuro-divergences or neurodiverse conditions have much more to offer. With the perceived deficits, also come a lot of gifts, that are possibly submerged but with the right environment they can be unleashed and when we do that, we create MAGIC!!

Orchvate Proposes Neurodiversity Doctrine Triad

  • Avoid over emphasis on labels.
  • Neuro-divergent Individuals have the right to be the way they are different.
  • Create an empathetic setting that accommodates and encourages all to work to their full potential.

Awareness and Sensitization

Online workshops by Neuro diverse team of facilitators to enhance awareness & empathy.

These also emphasize on the “what’s in it for me” to employees and identify who would be interested in forming a core ND Employee Support Group.

ND Ally Certification

Focus groups with ESG members, team members, Team Leads and Managers of ND Hires.

These ensure safe work environments. Are conducted by ND Co Trainers to provide hands on experience of working in neurodiverse teams, through simulations.

Hiring

Competency mapping of targeted roles and identification of Behavioural Indicators.

Competency assessment tools designed to assess and screen ND individuals. Partner with Hiring companies to identify the right fits.

Coaching

One on one and group coaching interventions with ND employees to ensure they are well integrated.

Individual Development Plans are laid down, progress reviewed and feedback is shared with employees and their Managers.

Employee Parent Support

Platform for employees, whose children are neurodivergent.

Webinars to empower them and address parenting challenges.

Build a self-help community within the organization, that results in employee engagement CSR initiatives in the ND space.
